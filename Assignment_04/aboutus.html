How it Works ?

Buyers Can either Purchase or visit the books.
Buyers can either search for specific books using our “Search” box or alternatively they can browse the various categories and select something which they might be interested in. You can also do an “Advanced Search” or select the “Browse Books” option to define cost parameters for your search. Once a buyer clicks on a book name, a “Book Details” page will open up listing all the available copies of that book sorted by their respective condition. To get detailed info on a particular copy, simply click the “More Info” button next to the book and all the relevant details will be displayed in the top half of the window, including price, condition and any seller comments. For Purchase a copy, Click button “Add to Cart” and you will be guided through to the checkout process.
